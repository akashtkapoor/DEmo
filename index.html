The Team

Guided by passion,
shaped by seasons

Antoine Moreau
Executive Chef & Founder
Trained at Taillevent and Noma before returning to his native countryside to open Maison Verdant in 2009. James Beard nominated 2022.
Clara Reyes
Head Chef, Pastry
Clara's desserts celebrate the forgotten arts of preservation — lacto-fermented fruits, aged creams, and seasonal preserves made in our larder.
Marcus Chen
Head Sommelier
Marcus sources only natural, low-intervention wines that share our kitchen's values — biodynamic farms, honest winemakers, living bottles.
